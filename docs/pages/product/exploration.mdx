# Exploration

Exploration tools help you analyze data, build reports, and share insights with your team.

<SuccessBox>

Exploration tools are only available in Cube Cloud.

</SuccessBox>

## Exploration tools in Cube Cloud

In Cube Cloud, you can:

- Use [Explore][ref-explore] to interactively explore your semantic layer with a
drag-and-drop interface, creating visualizations, pivot tables, and tables without
writing code.
- Use [Analytics Chat][ref-analytics-chat] to explore your data using natural
language questions and get AI-powered insights instantly.
- Use [Workbooks][ref-workbooks] to build reports with an AI agent, organize the
results of your analysis, and share insights with your team.
